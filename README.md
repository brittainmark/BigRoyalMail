# BigRoyalMail
Zen Cart Big Royal Mail shipping options

This module comprises of separate shipping modules and tariffs for the shipping methods available from the post office:

Royal Mail 1st & 2nd Class<br>
Royal Mail 1st & 2nd Class Large Letter<br>
Royal Mail 1st & 2nd Class Small Parcel<br>
Royal Mail 1st & 2nd Class Medium Parcel<br>
Royal Mail Special Delivery Next Day service 9am and 1pm and Saturday<br>
Royal Mail Airmail<br>
Royal Mail Airmail International Tracked<br>
Royal Mail Airmail International Tracked & Signed<br>
Parcel Force Worldwide UK delivery: 48hr, 24hr, AM, by 10am<br>
Parcel Force Worldwide International delivery: Express, Priority, Ireland Express.

You can also use attributes to exclude certain postage methods for a product. 

For documentation see [wiki](https://github.com/brittainmark/BigRoyalMail/wiki)
